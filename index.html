About

Hello! I'm Clem, a Junior Developer

I recently graduated from the General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive bootcamp and I'm currently looking for my first Junior Developer role in London, UK. I create Full-Stack apps, my strengths are JavaScript, HTML, CSS and React . I'm also confident with a bunch of other technologies.

Working for a SAAS company as a Customer Success Manager, I have sharpened my communication skills and work closely with the product team. Working with them has given me a good understanding of the product development lifecycle. In doing so, I became interested in the engineering that went into the development of the product.

I began programming after reading an article about Python and started with 'Learn Python the Hard Way' which gave me a foundation to object-oriented programming and the command line. My favorite project was a text-adventure game for the terminal to hone my understanding of learning concepts. I made it absurd and laughed the whole way through and thus began my coding path. From there, I wanted to code full time and so enrolled in a bootcamp to accelerate my learning process.

During the bootcamp I created and collaborated on 4 projects: a Canvas JavaScript game, a front end consuming the Spotify podcast API, a full-stack MERN e-commerce store and a React, Django and PostgreSQL exercise dashboard.

My instructors describe me as ambitious and unafraid of working with new concepts and technologies (or lack of coding stage-fright). I would describe myself as confident and driven, able to work well as an individual contributor and as part of a team.

My next project will be using Firebase and TypeScript to develop an app using the Test Driven Development method and Jest testing suite.

Outside of coding I follow the Tampa Bay Lightning (NHL team) and play and record guitar. I also enjoy swimming, going on bike rides and rollerblading.

Lastly, I'm a big music and podcast fan: in 2021 I listened to 110,000+ minutes (76 full days) of audio in 2021 on Spotify. The main playlists I listen to I are my giant mixes All Killer, No Filler and Fun Electro. My favorite podcast is the Tim Ferris Show and I've recently added Modern Finance to my rotation.
